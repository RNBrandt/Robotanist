Bryophytes Represented in the UC Herbarium

This list of bryophyte taxa represented in the University Herbarium (UC) was generated from a list of folders in the collection. It represents an interim measure while a complete specimen inventory database is under development. We hope it will prove helpful in the meantime to indicate taxa available for study or loans. In our filing system, California collections are always placed into a separate folder (the names in Gold are all the taxa with a California folder). Other geographic splits are used only for taxa with many specimens, thus if no geographic information is shown, the folder includes all specimens regardless of location.
If you have a specific request, please contact the curator of the Bryophyte Herbarium.

Names in Gold are bryophytes represented in CALIFORNIA.
